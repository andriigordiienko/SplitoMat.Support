## Why people switch to SplitoMat

- **Everything free, no limits.** Splitwise now caps how many expenses you can add
  per day on the free tier and puts a timer between entries; receipt scanning,
  currency conversion, default splits and search moved behind Splitwise Pro.
  SplitoMat does all of that for free, with no daily limits.
- **Things no one else has.** A shared prepaid **kitty** for trips/flats, a
  **default income-based split** per group (set 60/40 once), **Year in Review**,
  and **export of just your own share** for your personal budget app.
- **Private by design.** Your data lives in the **EU (Frankfurt)**, with **no ad or
  behavioral trackers**, and you can export or delete everything at any time.
- **Native and everywhere.** A clean SwiftUI iPhone app plus **Apple Watch**,
  **home & lock-screen widgets**, and **Siri** — in English, German, Spanish,
  Russian and Ukrainian.
