# Switch from Splitwise in 2 minutes

Splitwise now paywalls basics you used to get for free — a daily limit on adding
expenses, receipt scanning, currency conversion, charts. **SplitoMat is free,
with no ads and no paywalled basics** — and you can bring your whole history
across so you don't lose a thing.

Here's the two-minute move.

## 1. Export your group from Splitwise (1 min)

You need to do this on the **Splitwise website** (the export isn't in the phone
app):

1. Open [secure.splitwise.com](https://secure.splitwise.com) and sign in.
2. Open the group you want to move.
3. Click the **gear / ⚙️ (Group settings)** in the top-right of the group.
4. Choose **Export as spreadsheet** → **CSV**.
5. A `.csv` file downloads (e.g. `Trip_to_Lisbon.csv`). AirDrop or email it to
   your iPhone, or save it to Files / iCloud Drive.

> Do this once per group. Each group is its own CSV.

## 2. Import into SplitoMat (1 min)

1. In SplitoMat, go to **Settings → Import from Splitwise**.
2. Tap **Choose CSV file** and pick the file you just exported.
3. On the review screen:
   - Give the new group a name (we pre-fill it from the file name).
   - Under **Who are you?**, pick your own name so expenses land on your
     account. Everyone else is added as a name-only **guest** — no account or
     invite needed.
4. Tap **Import expenses**. Done.

We rebuild **every expense** — who paid, how it was split, payments, categories —
so your **balances match Splitwise to the cent**. Multi-payer expenses are
handled too.

## What comes across

| From Splitwise | In SplitoMat |

| Who paid — single **and** multiple payers | ✅ Rebuilt |

## After importing

- **Invite the others** so they see the group on their phones: open the group →
  share the invite link. They can also join as guests first.
- Keep splitting for free — no daily limit, receipt scanning, live currency
  rates, and charts are all included.

## Notes & limits

- The importer reads Splitwise's **CSV** ("Export as spreadsheet"). If you only
  have an Excel/PDF, re-export as CSV.
- Comments, reactions and receipt images aren't in Splitwise's export, so they
  can't be imported — but everything that affects **money and balances** is.
- Everything is parsed **on your device**. The CSV isn't uploaded anywhere.

## Trouble?

- Balances look off? Double-check you picked **yourself** under "Who are you?".
- File won't import? Make sure it's the **CSV** export, not a screenshot or PDF.
