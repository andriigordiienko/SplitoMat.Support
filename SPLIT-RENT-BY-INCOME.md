# Split rent fairly — by income, automatically

If you and your flatmates (or you and your partner) earn different amounts,
splitting rent 50/50 isn't fair. The fair way is **proportional to income**: if
one earns €3,000 and the other €2,000, rent should split **60/40**.

Most apps make you compute that percentage yourself and re-type it on every
expense. SplitoMat does it for you — **set it once, and every bill splits itself
that way from then on**, including recurring bills like rent, utilities and
internet.

## Set it up once (30 seconds)

1. Open your group → **Settings** (the gear).
2. Under **Default split**, turn on **Uneven split**.
3. Tap **Income**.
4. Enter each person's income (e.g. `3000` and `2000`). SplitoMat shows the
   resulting share live (e.g. **60% / 40%**).
5. Tap **Save split**.

That's it. Every new expense in the group is now pre-split by those proportions.

## What happens next

- **Every new expense** opens already split 60/40 (or whatever your incomes
  imply) — no typing percentages, no mental math.
- **Recurring bills** (rent, electricity, internet) use the same split every
  month automatically. Set the rent up once as a recurring expense and it splits
  correctly forever.
- **Any single expense can still be changed** — split the groceries 60/40 but
  the pizza someone treated you to 100/0. The default is a starting point, not a
  cage.

## Prefer shares instead of income?

Not everyone wants to share their salary. Under **Default split → Uneven
split**, choose **Shares** instead of **Income** and enter simple weights (e.g.
`2` and `1` for a two-thirds / one-third split, or by room size). Same result,
no incomes involved.

## Why this is fair (and why we built it)

Splitting equally when incomes differ quietly overcharges the person who earns
less. Proportional splitting is the standard fair approach for rent among people
with unequal incomes. Splitwise put *saving* an unequal default behind its Pro
paywall, and the free favourites (Tricount, Splid) don't do recurring bills at
all — so nobody made "set your income split once and let rent handle itself" easy
and free. We did.

## Trouble?

- Don't see **Uneven split**? Make sure you're in the group's **Settings**, not
  the expense screen.
- Split looks wrong? Check the incomes/shares add up the way you expect — the
  live percentage tells you before you save.
